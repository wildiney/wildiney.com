Privacy Policy

1. Data controller

Wildiney Di Masi, reachable at wildiney@gmail.com, is the data controller for personal data collected on this site, in accordance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018).

2. Data collected and purposes

2.1 Analytics cookies (with your consent)

When you accept cookies, we collect anonymous browsing data through:

  • Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager): pages visited, session duration, traffic source, and navigation behavior. Data processed by Google LLC in the United States.
  • Microsoft Clarity: heatmaps, session recordings, and click tracking. Data processed by Microsoft Corporation in the United States.

Legal basis: consent (LGPD art. 7, I).

2.2 Contact form

When you submit a message, we collect your name, email address, and message content. This data is transmitted to formsubmit.co, which forwards it to the controller's email, and is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Legal basis: legitimate interest (LGPD art. 7, IX).

3. Data processors

  • Google LLC — Google Analytics / Tag Manager. Privacy policy.
  • Microsoft Corporation — Microsoft Clarity. Privacy policy.
  • formsubmit.co — form delivery. Privacy policy.

4. Data retention

  • Analytics data: retained per each platform's policy (Google: 14 months default; Clarity: 13 months).
  • Contact messages: kept as long as needed to respond and archive the communication.

5. Your rights (LGPD art. 18)

You have the right to confirm processing, access, correct, anonymize, block, or delete your data, request portability, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any right, email wildiney@gmail.com.

6. Withdrawing consent

You can withdraw your analytics consent at any time by clearing site data in your browser settings. Withdrawal does not affect processing carried out prior to the revocation.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: wildiney@gmail.com.
